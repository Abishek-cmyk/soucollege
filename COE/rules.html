RULES AND REGULATIONS INSIDE THE CAMPUS RAGGING AND EVE TEASING ARE STRICTLY PROHIBITED.
THEY ARE OFFENCES AND PUNISHABLE UNDER PROHIBITION OF RAGGING ACT 1997

INSIDE THE CAMPUS

  • All students must wear identity cards, when they are inside the college Campus.
  • Students shall enter the classes on time and late-comers will lose attendance. However they'll be allowed to attend the classes with the permission of the HOD.
  • Students shall not make noise while leaving the classroom.
  • The first hour of each day will start with prayer.
  • Students are expected to give due respect to teachers when they enter the classroom.
  • Loitering and making noise in the corridors during class hours must be avoided.
  • Students shall maintain discipline and decorum inside the classrooms even in the absence of the teacher.
  • Severe punishment will be given to those students who cause damage to the college property. Therefore students shall handle electrical fittings, laboratory equipments, computers, library books and other college properties carefully.
  • No visitor (friend or family member) is allowed to meet the student during class hours without the permission of the HOD.
  • Whistling and hooting are serious offences. Students must avoid these practices inside the campus especially during college functions.
  • Students are not allowed to take part in the social / family functions of their classmates during working hours.
  • Students are allowed to go on tours / one day trips only after getting the permission from the parents, the HOD and the Principal.
  • Students found smoking, using drugs or alcohol inside the campus or during functions will be expelled from the college.
  • Students should not bring mobile phones to the college.
  • Organising and participating in unauthorised meetings / functions related to our college is punishable.
  • Students are asked to receive their marksheet from the respective HOD with their parents.

DRESS CODE

Every student of the college shall following the dress code

  • Wear modest, decent and acceptable dress.
  • Wearing T-Shirts and Black shirts are not permitted.
  • Girls are expected to wear sarees or chudithar with shawl properly pinned.

Under the Educational Rules of the Government, the Principal has full powers to impose fine, suspend or expel the students from the college, for the violation of any one of these rules.

ATTENDANCE

  • Attendance will be marked at the beginning of every hour.
  • No Student will be allowed to absent himself or herself from the 'college without submitting leave letter. He/ She must apply for leave in advance, stating explicitly the reasons for his / her absence. In cases where absence is due to unforeseen circumstances, application for leave should be submitted on the first day of return to the college.
  • All leave letters should be countersigned by the parent or guardian in the case of day scholars and in the case of resident students by the deputy warden concerned.
  • No student shall absent himself/ herself from internal tests without the prior permission.
  • Students should take tests seriously. No student will be permitted to leave the hall 10 Minutes before the end of the test.
  • The details of fine for every month will be displayed on the Office Notice Board. The fine should be paid within a fortnight
  • The name of the student who absents himself / herself without leave application for a peroid of ten days will be removed from college nominal roll
  • Attandance certificate can be held in the case of the student who fail to attend at least 75% of the lecture or practical classes in any subject during the academic year

College Timing 9.00 AM to 2.30 PM

OFF CAMPUS

  • Students are expected to behave decently in public places like bus stands, bus stops and during bus travel. Any incident detrimental to the interest of the college will be viewed seriously.
  • Crowding and indulging in undesirable activities near the college main entrance, causing nuisance to the public will be seriously dealt with.

SPECIFIC RULE

  • The name of the student who is continuously absent for fifteen days will be removed from roll and he / she wil be permitted to attend the classes only after meeting the principal with parents.
