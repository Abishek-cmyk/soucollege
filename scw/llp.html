Library and Laboratory Policies

Library and Reading Room

  • College library follows the Open Access System.
  • Strict silence shall be maintained in the library.
  • Students are allowed to use the library during working hours.

Library Rules and Regulations

  • Each student receives book issue tokens as per rules.
  • Reference books should not be taken out of the library.
  • Students must return books on or before the due date.
  • No marking, underlining, or damaging of books is allowed.
  • Books must be returned in good condition.
  • Late return will incur a fine per day.
  • Lost books must be replaced or paid for by the student.
  • Library staff and students must sign the library register when entering.

Laboratory Rules and Regulations

  • Students must perform only permitted experiments.
  • Misuse of chemicals or instruments is punishable.
  • Apparatus must be handled carefully.
  • Breakage must be reported immediately to staff.
  • Damaged items must be replaced by the student responsible.
  • Students should maintain neat, accurate records.
  • All practical work must be completed before appearing for examinations.
The bonafide certificate for practical work will be issued only if the student’s attendance and performance in the laboratory is satisfactory.
