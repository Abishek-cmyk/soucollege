About Our Principal

Capt. Dr. K.R. Srinivasan is the Principal with a rich experience of 24 years in the field of education. With Economics as his core subject, his career spans over a considerable period in various capacities like Head, Department of Economics, Associate in NCC, IQAC Coordinator, Controller of Examinations, Member of College Development Council, Member in Autonomous Committee etc. with many milestones to his credit. He has completed his B.A. (Economics), M.A. (Hons in Economics) , M.Phil. (Economics) and M.Phil. (Mgt) and M.B.A. from Madurai Kamaraj University, Madurai. He has also cleared State Level Eligibility Test (SLET) in 1999.He started his teaching career as a lecturer in 2000. He obtained his Ph.D., degree in 2014 and has published 25 research articles in various journals at national and international level.He has attended 67 seminars, conferences and workshops to his credit. Capt. Dr. K.R. Srinivasan has been serving since 2006 in various profiles. Under his able guidance, six NCC cadets attended RDC camp, three NCC Cadets attended TSC camp ,one cadet attended IDC camp and many NCC Cadets got placed in various uniformed services. A visionary by nature, he is in the process of mentoring and guiding the students and staff members to meet the institutional goals and objectives.

Principals Message

The college has rendered 57 years of relentless quality services to the society in the area of education, in the fields of Arts and Science, Management Studies and Computer Applications.

The College has well experienced and highly motivated faculty and administrative staff to make sure that the students enhance their learning skills, interpersonal skills, communication skills, technical skills and social skills which will mould them into well rounded educated citizen. The autonomous status grants the college academic freedom to introduce new UG courses like Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing & Cyber Security and to constantly review the curriculum and update as per the corporate requirements which in turn ensures that all our students become suitable to the corporate expectations when they graduate from our College.

The AICTE Affiliated courses like MBA and MCA with excellent academic records and placements have topped the institution.The notable features of Sourashtra College are adopting the Outcome Based teaching learning process, academic discipline, student centric practices in the laboratory and practical sessions, project based subject learning, study material,internship opportunities and providing various skill training for facing interviews, personality development programmes and active participation in the various seminars, conferences, activities for life skills, festivals, yoga etc.The four research departments encourage the scholars to do research, pre-research activities and to publish articles in reputed journals.
